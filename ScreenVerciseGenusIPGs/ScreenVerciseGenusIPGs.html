Battery Longevity
Designed with patient comfort in mind, the Vercise Genus DBS System features small, compact IPGs without compromising battery longevity.
The Vercise Genus R16 is a rechargeable IPG built to provide up to 25 years* of DBS therapy. Charging the device is simple and can be done as infrequently as once a month1.
The Vercise rechargeable batteries
contain proprietary lithium ion Zero Volt™ technology that is designed to maintain battery capacity for several years. This same technology also protects the battery from degradation if depleted.
*The battery life is dependent on the stimulation settings and conditions.
